Where do you fit in ?

Sports Science Aspirant

The ones who are fascinated by this field and wish to make it their career. You would -

•Acquire fundamental knowledge about sports science and its various disciplines

•Get a chance to assist and intern with sports science professionals and students to understand this field better

•Insights into the admission process for several sports science courses .


Sports Science Student

Individuals currently pursuing under-graduation or post-graduation can learn more about

•The current scenario of sports science in India

•Prevailing career opportunities for sports scientists

•The application of theoretical knowledge in practical settings

•Guidance on future prospects from established professionals

Sports Science Professional

If you are currently working as a sports scientist, you can

•Share your experiences with the upcoming sports scientists and also build a strong professional relationship with the other practicing sports scientists

•Share ideas, resources and stay up to date with the latest practices in the field

•Get connected with sports scientists abroad incase you wish to pursue higher studies in the near future

Why the SES Network ?

A Connecting Platform for Sports Professionals

Henry Ford once said, “Coming together is a beginning, staying together is progress, and working together is success.”

The SES network is aimed to be built based on our motto-
“Of Sports Scientists, By Sports Scientists, For Sports Scientists!”
A robust community, consisting of members who are well informed and up to date with the current practices and happenings in the field of sports, is of utmost importance in order to spread awareness amongst the general population.

We believe this forum will benefit all those associated in many ways!
