Get to know about The SES

The science of sports and exercise is currently booming in our country and the pace at which it is expected to prosper especially post India’s scintillating performances at the world stage is extremely promising.

Sports and high performance has become immensely competitive like never before with countries across the globe using cutting edge infrastructure, technology and research to push boundaries and sporting records. The field of Sports and Exercise Science in India is only in its infancy and we strongly believe in developing the same by improving the quality and standards of our practices.

The SES Network is committed to building a high calibre networking platform for budding and established Sports Scientists in India where one can expect free flow of knowledge, guidance and resources from experienced professionals and this includes a series of bi-monthly talks as well!

If you are a sports scientist or someone who is aspiring to get into this field, register now!

Gayatri Raghavan

Sports Science Professional-Kinesiologist

Gayatri’s passion for sports and dance has carried through over the years and she is immensely grateful that she could convert this into her dream career by pursuing sports and exercise sciences. It is her vision to develop the sporting culture and high performance sports system in India from the grassroots up to the elite level. As a stepping stone to this she founded The SES Network to spread awareness on how science is an integral part of sport development and how the various professionals could come together to form a robust sporting ecosystem. At present she is pursuing her Masters in Kinesiology at the University of British Columbia, Vancouver.
