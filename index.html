Who we are

Get to know about The SES Network

The science of sports and exercise is currently booming in our country and the pace at which it is expected to prosper especially post India’s scintillating performance at the Tokyo 2020 Olympics is extremely promising.

Sports and high performance have become immensely competitive like never before with countries across the globe using cutting edge infrastructure, technology and research to push boundaries and sporting records. The field of Sports and Exercise Science in India is only in its infancy and we strongly believe in developing the same by improving the quality and standards of our practices.

The SES Network is committed to building a high calibre networking platform for budding and established Sports Scientists in India where one can expect free flow of knowledge, guidance and resources from experienced professionals and this includes a series of bi-monthly talks as well!
